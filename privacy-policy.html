Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02 June 2026

Koupan respects your privacy. This policy explains how we may collect and use information when you use our website or mobile app.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, mobile number, coupon scan details, reward history, payout details and basic device or usage information required to operate the service.

How We Use Information

We use information to verify users, validate coupons, prevent duplicate claims, process rewards, improve service quality and provide support.

Payments and Payouts

For reward payouts, information may be shared with authorized payment or payout partners only as required to complete the transaction.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information. No system is 100% secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@koupan.in.
